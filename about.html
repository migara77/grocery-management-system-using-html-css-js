About Our System

Our Grocery Management System is designed to simplify the way groceries are bought, managed, and billed. Whether you're a store owner or a customer, this system offers a modern, user-friendly interface that makes grocery shopping easy and efficient.

Features

User Login: Secure authentication to keep your data safe.

Product Catalog: Browse products easily by categories.

Cart System: Add, edit, and manage your selected items seamlessly.

Billing & Payment Simulation: Generate bills and simulate payments quickly.
